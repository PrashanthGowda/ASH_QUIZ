How To Play:

  1. Click on “Play Quiz”.
  2. KINDLY DONOT REFRESH THE PAGE OR DONOT PRESS BACK BUTTON AT ANY POINT OF TIME DURING THE QUIZ OR DURING REGISTRATION.
    WHILE USING MOBILE PLEASE DON’T SCROLL DOWN SWIFTLY AS IT WILL REFRESH THE PAGE.
  3. Enter all the required fields such as Name, Age, Gender, State, Mobile No. & Email ID. Before saving the details kindly verify once & Click on “Save & Continue”.
  4. Read the Terms & Conditions properly before starting the quiz & Click on  “Agree & Start Quiz”.
  5. Select the correct answer & Click on “Submit & next”.
  6. Before submitting each answer, you can change the options. Once you click on the “Submit & next” button you cannot go back and change it.
  7. Winning participants must furnish their bank details upon which prize money will be credited to their account. The winners will also be provided with a Prize Winner’s Certificate which they can download.
  8. Upon completion of the Quiz, those who have not answered the necessary number of questions to win a prize can also download a Certificate of Participation.

Terms And Conditions:

  1. This is a timed quiz with 10 questions to be answered in 3 minutes (180 seconds).
  2. These questions will be randomly picked from the question bank regarding the Life and Teachings of Swami Vivekananda.
  3. Any individual can participate only once in the quiz.
  4. The mobile number and email address of the participant are mandatory and cannot be used more than once to participate in the quiz.
  5. Winners will be awarded Cash prizes of Rs. 200/- for 8 and 9 correct answers, and Rs. 300/- for answering all 10 questions correctly.
  6. Ramakrishna Math, Bengaluru is providing a Digital Certificate for all the participants and a Prize Winner’s Certificate for those who win a prize.
  7. The selected winners must have a bank account and the prize money will be directly credited to their account.
  8. Bank details required for winners:
    • Account holder’s name
    • Bank Name
    • Account Number
    • City
    • Branch
    • IFSC Code
    • Mobile Number
  9. Prize Winners will not be allowed to claim prize money from any of the Ramakrishna Math and Ramakrishna Mission centres across India.
  10. Prize winners should contact Ramakrishna Math, Bengaluru directly for any further clarifications.
  11. Ramakrishna Math, Bengaluru has every right to cancel the Prize if any participant is involved in unfair and illegal practices.
  12. If same account number is used to claim the prize more than once, Ramakrishna Math, Bengaluru has right to verify before crediting to their account.
