How To Play

  1. Click on “Play Quiz”.
  2. NOTE: KINDLY DONOT REFRESH THE PAGE AT ANY POINT OF TIME DURING THE QUIZ OR DURING REGISTRATION.
    WHILE USING MOBILE PLEASE DON’T SCROLL DOWN SWIFTLY AS IT WILL REFRESH THE PAGE.
  3. Enter all the required fields such as Name, Age, Gender, State, Mobile No & Email ID. Before saving the details kindly verify once & Click on “Save & Continue”.
  4. Read the Quiz Rules properly & Click on “Agree & Start Quiz”.
  5. Select the correct answer & Click on “Submit & next”.

QUIZ RULES:

  1. We are allowing the participant to play quiz for only one time. Please do not try to play for multiple times.
  2. DONOT REFRESH THE PAGE AT ANY POINT OF TIME DURING THE QUIZ.
  3. Each player will get 10 questions on various topics, which should be answered within 150 seconds (2.5 Minutes).
  4. Before submitting your answer you can change the options. Once you Click on the submit you cannot change it again.
  5. If you answer 8 questions correctly you will be receiving the prize voucher for Rs.200/-
  6. If you answer all the 10 questions correctly you will be receiving the prize voucher for Rs.300/-
  7. The winners are requested download the voucher and show that voucher at the allotted counter at the bookstall.
  8. The Winner can buy Books or other articles worth the Voucher amount. Winner cannot claim any cash for the said voucher amount.
  9. This voucher cannot be used as discount coupon or cannot be adjusted part of any other bill.
  10. This voucher is valid for only one day i.e on Sunday 06.12.2019
